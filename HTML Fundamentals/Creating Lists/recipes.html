Hot Cocoa Recipe

Ingredients

  • T Wiren Brain Coffe Hot cocoa Mix
  • 8 oz Milk
  • Marshmallows (optional)

Steps

  1. Scoop Hot Cocoa Mix into a mug.
  2. Heat milk on the stove or in the microwave to just below a boil
  3. Pour milk into the mug, stirring gently as you pour
  4. Stir until mix is fully dissolved in the milk
  5. Top with Marshmallows if you must

Cocoa Facts

Do you Know the difference?
Cocoa
Cocoa is the powdered substance left after processing cacao beans and has little or now cocoa butter
Chocolate
Chocolate is basically cocoa whith the cocoa butter and often sugar and milk.
